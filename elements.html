The First Nation

Air Nomads

The Air Nomads were a peaceful civilization of monks and nuns who lived in four Air Temples scattered across the world. They roamed the skies on flying bison, renouncing material attachment and dedicating their lives to spiritual enlightenment. Airbending, the gentlest yet most evasive of all arts, reflects their philosophy of freedom.

Spiritual Freedom Nomadic Peaceful
Philosophy

Non-attachment and harmony with all living things guide the Air Nomad way of life.

Element Style

Airbending is evasive and circular, using redirection over force — a dance with the wind.

🌊

Polar Guardians

Water Tribe

Split between the North and South Poles, the Water Tribe are resilient survivors of the harshest climates. Their connection to the moon and ocean gives their bending remarkable depth — from crashing tidal waves to delicate healing arts. The Southern Water Tribe warriors are known for their tenacity and loyalty.

Resilient Healing Loyal Adaptive
Philosophy

Flow like water — adapt to any obstacle, change form, yet always find your way forward.

Element Style

Waterbending moves in fluid arcs, shifting between offense and healing with equal grace.

🪨

The Mighty Realm

Earth Kingdom

The largest nation in the world, the Earth Kingdom is as vast and diverse as the element it commands. From the mighty walls of Ba Sing Se to the labyrinthine caves of the Badgermoles, earthbenders embody strength, stubbornness, and perseverance. They do not yield — they endure.

Strong Stubborn Enduring Diverse
Philosophy

Stand firm. Like a mountain, meet force with force and never retreat from what matters.

Element Style

Earthbending is powerful and direct — powerful stances, rock strikes, and impenetrable walls.

🔥

The Fierce Empire

Fire Nation

Powerful, disciplined, and driven by ambition, the Fire Nation harnessed the sun's energy to fuel a century-long war of conquest. Yet within this empire of power lies a capacity for honor, redemption, and extraordinary courage. Firebending is fueled by breath and will — an art of pure, focused energy.

Passionate Disciplined Ambitious Honorable
Philosophy

Power comes from within — breath, willpower, and the relentless pursuit of greatness.

Element Style

Firebending is aggressive and explosive — sharp kicks and strikes that unleash pure force.
